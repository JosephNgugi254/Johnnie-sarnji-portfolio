About me

Hello, I'm Johnnie Sarnji, a seasoned filmmaker, cinematographer, videographer, social media influencer, and digital content creator with close to a decade of experience in the industry. My passion for visual storytelling has led me to work with some of the biggest brands and companies, including Inchcape Kenya, Muthokinju paints & cement, Denri Africa, Bespoke City, KCB Group, Hunter's Choice Kenya among others.
In addition to my professional work, I also have a YouTube channel with over 285k subscribers, where I share my expertise and connect with a global audience, further establishing my presence as a social media influencer.

Throughout my career, I've collaborated with a myriad of internet personalities, content creators, and influencers in Kenya, such as MoyaDavid1, Prince Indah, Musa Jakadala, Brian Mutinda, Ms_Musau, OnlyLitBoy, Flirty Carlos, Sandra Dacha, and Jemutai Churchill. Currently, I manage GkSerkal and handle videography for Oga Obinna, along with working on numerous other brand projects. My dedication to my craft has earned the trust of many, and I strive to continue creating impactful and engaging visual content that resonates with audiences.
Let's create something remarkable together.
